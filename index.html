Spidermando

A quick guide

The Origin of Spidermando

Peter Parker always wanted to become an military commando when he grew up. Ever since he was 5, he carried within himself a sense of duty and purpose: to become the best military commando there ever was. However, at military camp, a little mishap happened. While fighting pesky golden eagles for food one evening, a small red spider bit him and he was instantly put into a coma. However, when he reawakened, something wasn’t the same. He now had thousands of small hooks on his fingertips that allowed him to scale walls with ease! Also, he recently picked up a new trend where everyone wore a red mask on their face. But in the end, he decided he wouldn’t be just a regular “commando” anymore. Forever grateful for the spider that changed his life forever, he changed his name to a mixture of his two passions: Spidermando.

Our game includes two levels:
Level 1 is where Spidermando the GREEN GOBLIN, who terrorizes New York City.
Level 2 is where Spidermando clashes with VENOM, who Spidermando is just jealous of.

Controls

Move with WASD, without the S. Spidermando doesn’t need to move down since he did not get bitten by a groundhog.
Aim weapon with cursor, left click to shoot.

Spidermando

Starts with 100 health and 10 ammo. Can climb walls because of his spider abilities. To climb, just go next to a wall and he will start climbing. Can also jump in the air… sometimes.

Gunner

Enemy that shoots at Spidermando when he is on the same level as him, and does 4 damage if his bullet hits. Takes 10 shots to kill.

Green Goblin

Level one boss. Flies on the top of the screen when you reach the end of the level, does 20 damage per fireball when it hits Spidermando and takes 20 shots to kill.

Venom

Level two boss. This man is a bit harder to finish. Venom takes 50 shots to kill, and his projectiles deal 5 damage to you per shot. His bullets are also a bit faster than the Green Goblin's, so you better stay on your fingertips.

Spike Ball

Rolls slowly along the ground. Watch out! Takes 3 shots to destroy, and will do 10 damage if it hits Spidermando.

Cannon

Indestructible, spawns spike balls.

Health

This pickup gives you 25 health.

Ammo

This pickup gives you 10 ammo.

Spikes

Don’t touch them, they are pointy! They do continuous damage, but not a lot. You should still avoid them when possible.

Block

Reminds me of minesweeper.

End Flag

Touch it and win. Must beat boss first, though.
